How I work

From messy problem
to clear solution

01

Understand the problem

It all starts with the user. Through research, feedback, product reviews, usage patterns, and direct experience, I work to understand the underlying problem before exploring solutions.

02

Define & prioritise

Not every problem deserves immediate attention. I identify root causes, assess business and user impact, and prioritize opportunities based on value, urgency, and feasibility.

03

Propose & validate

I explore potential solutions, define product requirements, and validate assumptions through user feedback and testing before committing resources to development.

04

Ship & measure

Working closely with engineers and stakeholders, I help translate ideas into products, define success metrics, and measure outcomes to inform future decisions.
